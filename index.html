Contrast between the syllabus and becoming a software engineer.

Collaboration between people.
As the software engineer debunked this myth about not collaborting with people, she mentioned that in most of the project you have to work with someone, and pairing up in class plays a vital role in developing the skills to communicate and express your ideas to other people. Moreover, this also improves how you treat other people, and makes you more eligible to pick up social ques and norms.
Self assessment and peer inspection.
As mentioned by the engineer, once you are done with your work you hand it to someone else to take a look, then they try to debug it. This encourages teamwork, helps other provide a feedback on your work and an insight on how to improve it This is more or less the same thing we will practice in class as peer assesment is a vital part of our curriculum we will be able to replicate real-world issues within the classroom
Learning soft skills and Technical Skills
In this class we will learn numerous computer languages, and work on different projects. Some of these projects are to be completed in pairs, which not only provides us with the technical skills to do that project, but working with someone else aids us in enhancing our soft skills such as communication and expression of ideas.
Role models are important
1. Some people might already have their role models and look up to them, but these role models may not be in their field of work.
2 .Furthermore, one can always have more than one role model, therefore, during this course there should be seminars where computer scientists who have their own start-up or work for other firms come in and talk to us about this field.
3. In addition, enlighten us on what steps we can take before entering our professional life to gain a competitive edge over others.
