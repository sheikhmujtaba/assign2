Contrast between the syllabus and becoming a software engineer.

Collaboration between people.
As the software engineer debunked this myth about not collaborting with people, she mentioned that ini most of the project you have to work with someone, and pairing up in class plays a vital role in developing the skills to communicate and express your ideas to other people. Moreover, this also improves how you treat other people, and makes you more eligible to pick up social ques and norms.
Self assessment and peer inspection.
As mentioned by the engineers, once you are done with your work you hand it to someone else to take a look, then they try to debug it. This encourages teamwork, helps other provide a feedback on your work and an insight on how to improve it
